# Croko - Business Overview

## Company Description
Croko is a Colombian business specializing in pregnancy makeup kits and belly painting services, targeting expecting mothers and families in Medellín and surrounding areas.

## Mission
To transform pregnancy into a celebration by creating meaningful family bonding experiences and unforgettable memories through safe, professional belly painting activities.

## Vision
To become the leading pregnancy experience brand in Colombia, helping families create their first precious memories with their unborn babies.

## Business Model
- **E-commerce**: Online sales of pregnancy makeup kits
- **Service-based**: Home visit belly painting sessions
- **Event services**: Baby shower packages and family photography

## Location & Market
- **Primary Market**: Medellín, Colombia
- **Secondary Markets**: Antioquia region
- **Target Expansion**: Major Colombian cities (Bogotá, Cali, Barranquilla)

## Company Values
- **Family First**: Every activity strengthens family bonds
- **Safety**: Only certified, safe products for mother and baby
- **Cultural Respect**: Honoring Colombian family traditions
- **Memory Creation**: Building lasting memories before birth
- **Emotional Wellness**: Supporting maternal mental health

## Competitive Advantages
1. First-mover advantage in pregnancy experience market
2. Culturally relevant approach to Colombian families
3. Complete family experience (not just mother-focused)
4. Professional-quality products for home use
5. Mental health support integration
6. Bilingual content and support
