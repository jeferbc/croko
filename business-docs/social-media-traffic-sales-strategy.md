**Content Strategy**:
1. **Product Reveal Videos**: "Unboxing Croko kits" with website links
2. **Before/After Transformations**: 15-30 second reveals driving curiosity
3. **Trending Challenges**: #BellyArtChallenge linking to tutorial page
4. **Story Completion**: Multi-part videos ending with "Link in bio"
5. **User-Generated Content**: Repost customer videos with product tags

**Traffic Drivers**:
- Bio link rotation (change weekly)

- Live sessions mentioning website exclusives
