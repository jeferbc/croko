## Crisis Communication Guidelines

### Potential Issues & Responses

#### Product Safety Concerns
- **Response**: Immediate acknowledgment, transparent investigation
- **Message**: "Safety is our top priority. We're investigating and will update you within 24 hours."
- **Action**: Provide detailed safety certifications and test results

#### Service Quality Complaints
- **Response**: Personal apology, immediate resolution offer
- **Message**: "We're sorry we didn't meet your expectations. Let us make this right."
- **Action**: Refund, re-service, compensation as appropriate

#### Competitive Attacks
- **Response**: Focus on own values, avoid direct confrontation
- **Message**: "We focus on creating beautiful family experiences for our customers."
- **Action**: Highlight unique benefits, customer testimonials
