# Marketing Strategy & Growth Plan

## Marketing Objectives

### Short-term Goals (3-6 months)
- Increase website traffic by 150%
- Generate 50 qualified leads per month
- Achieve 25% increase in social media followers
- Establish email list of 500+ subscribers
- Launch Google Ads with 5% conversion rate

### Medium-term Goals (6-12 months)
- Expand to 3 additional Colombian cities
- Build network of 10 affiliate partners
- Create 50+ video content pieces
- Achieve 1,000+ email subscribers
- Generate COP $10M+ monthly revenue

### Long-term Goals (1-2 years)
- Become #1 pregnancy experience brand in Colombia
- Launch franchise/licensing model
- Develop app-based booking system
- Create pregnancy wellness content platform
- Expand to other Latin American markets

## Social Media Strategy

### Instagram (Primary Platform)
- **Content Mix**: 70% educational, 20% behind-scenes, 10% promotional
- **Posting Schedule**: Daily posts, 3-4 stories per day
- **Content Types**: Before/after photos, time-lapse videos, family testimonials
- **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix of popular and niche pregnancy hashtags
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Pregnancy bloggers, family influencers

### Facebook (Community Building)
- **Groups Strategy**: Join pregnancy groups, share valuable content
- **Event Promotion**: Baby shower packages, workshops
- **Video Content**: Live Q&A sessions, tutorial videos
- **Customer Service**: Response to inquiries and support

### YouTube (Education & Trust)
- **Channel Strategy**: Tutorial videos, customer stories
- **Content Calendar**: Weekly uploads, seasonal specials
- **SEO Optimization**: Keyword-rich titles, descriptions
- **Collaboration**: Guest appearances on parenting channels

### TikTok (Emerging Platform)
- **Content Focus**: Quick tutorials, trend participation
- **Target**: Younger pregnant women (22-28)
- **Trend Integration**: Pregnancy reveals, belly progression

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Google Ads
- **Campaign Types**: Search, Shopping, Display
- **Keywords**: High-intent pregnancy and baby shower terms
- **Landing Pages**: Specific pages for different products/services
- **Budget Allocation**: 60% search, 25% shopping, 15% display

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- **Objective**: Lead generation, website traffic, conversions
- **Targeting**: Pregnant women in Colombia, interests in family/parenting
- **Creative Strategy**: Video testimonials, before/after photos
- **Budget**: Start with COP $500k monthly, scale based on performance

### Retargeting Strategy
- Website visitors who didn't convert
- Video viewers on social media
- Email subscribers who haven't purchased
- Previous customers for repeat business
