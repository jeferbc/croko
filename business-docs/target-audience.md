# Target Audience & Customer Personas

## Primary Target Audience

### Main Customer: Expecting Mothers
- **Age**: 22-38 years old
- **Income**: Middle to upper-middle class (COP $2M - $8M monthly)
- **Location**: Medellín, Antioquia, Colombia
- **Education**: Secondary to university level
- **Lifestyle**: Family-oriented, social media active, values experiences over material goods

### Secondary Customers: Family Members
- **Partners/Spouses**: Involved fathers-to-be seeking bonding experiences
- **Grandparents**: Traditional family values, gift-givers
- **Siblings**: Children excited about new baby arrival
- **Friends**: Baby shower organizers and gift buyers

## Customer Personas

### Persona 1: "Carolina la Mamá Primeriza" (First-time Mom)
- **Age**: 26 years old
- **Occupation**: Marketing professional
- **Income**: COP $3.5M monthly
- **Pain Points**: Anxiety about motherhood, wants to involve partner
- **Motivations**: Creating memories, family bonding, Instagram-worthy moments
- **Buying Behavior**: Researches extensively, reads reviews, price-conscious but willing to pay for quality

### Persona 2: "Sofia la Mamá Experimentada" (Experienced Mom)
- **Age**: 32 years old
- **Occupation**: Teacher/Stay-at-home mom
- **Income**: COP $2.8M household
- **Pain Points**: Helping older children adjust to new sibling
- **Motivations**: Family tradition, sibling bonding, stress relief
- **Buying Behavior**: Quick decision maker, values convenience, repeat customer potential

### Persona 3: "Ana la Abuela Consentidora" (Doting Grandmother)
- **Age**: 55 years old
- **Occupation**: Retired/Business owner
- **Income**: COP $5M+ monthly
- **Pain Points**: Wants to be involved, create lasting memories
- **Motivations**: Family legacy, spoiling grandchildren, cultural traditions
- **Buying Behavior**: Gift purchaser, premium product preference, word-of-mouth influence

## Customer Journey

### Awareness Stage
- Pregnancy announcement
- Social media discovery
- Friend recommendations
- Google searches for "actividades embarazo Medellín"

### Consideration Stage
- Website research
- Instagram portfolio review
- Price comparison
- Reading testimonials
- Watching tutorial videos

### Decision Stage
- Product customization
- Booking consultation
- Purchase decision
- Scheduling service

### Retention Stage
- Experience sharing on social media
- Referral generation
- Repeat purchases for future pregnancies
- Community building

## Marketing Channels Priority

### High Impact
1. **Instagram**: Visual platform, perfect for before/after photos
2. **Facebook**: Community groups, targeted ads to pregnant women
3. **WhatsApp**: Direct communication, consultation booking
4. **Google Ads**: Capturing search intent

### Medium Impact
1. **YouTube**: Tutorial videos, testimonials
2. **Email Marketing**: Nurturing leads, retention
3. **Influencer partnerships**: Pregnancy/parenting bloggers
4. **Medical partnerships**: OB-GYN clinics, prenatal classes

### Low Impact (Long-term)
1. **TikTok**: Younger demographic growth
2. **LinkedIn**: B2B partnerships
3. **Traditional media**: Radio, local magazines
