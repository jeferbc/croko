# Products & Services Portfolio

## Core Product: Kit Maquillaje Prenatal

### Product Purpose
The Kit Maquillaje Prenatal is much more than a makeup product - it's a family experience designed to create the first precious memories between your growing family and your unborn baby.

### Core Benefits
- **First Family Interaction**: Creates the very first moment where the entire family can physically connect with the unborn baby
- **Memory Creation**: Transforms pregnancy into a celebration with beautiful photographs and unforgettable moments
- **Bonding Activity**: Brings families together in meaningful, creative activities
- **Emotional Connection**: Allows family members to feel baby's movements and responses
- **Stress Relief**: Provides relaxing, joyful activity for pregnant mothers
- **Cultural Celebration**: Honors Colombian tradition of celebrating pregnancy as a family journey

### Product Includes
- **2 plantillas personalizables**: Elige desde nuestro catálogo de 30 diseños o envíanos los tuyos (siempre con un único personaje)
- **4 pinceles profesionales**: Para diferentes técnicas de pintura
- **Pinturas corporales seguras**: Certificadas para piel sensible y embarazo
- **Videos guía paso a paso**: Para pintar la barriguita junto a familiares o amigos
- **Imágenes de referencia**: Catálogo completo para elegir diseño
